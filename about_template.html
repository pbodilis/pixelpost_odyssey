About

Who am I?

Hi there,
My name is Pierre Bodilis. I work as an IT Engineer in telecommunication in Paris, France. I lived for a while in the Meadowlands, very close to NYC, which may explain the numerous pictures from this city. I love to travel (oh well, who doesn't?). I'm always with a camera on my shoulder, "just in case". I fell into photography thanks to my former flatmate somewhere in September 2007. At first, I tried to comply with a publication rate of one picture a day, but this was way too demanding. After a year, I stepped down to a picture every 2 or 3 days, and I have been trying to keep up with this rate since.

All the pictures published on this blog are under by-nc-sa creative common. It is forbidden to use them without my authorization. If by any chance, you recognize yourself on one of those pictures, and you want me to remove it, just send me an email using the contact form in this page, and I'll do it straightaway.

Did I tell you I'm a big fan of Futurama?
